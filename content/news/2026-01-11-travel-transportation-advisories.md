Attendees should be aware of potential transportation disruptions in the days
leading up to FOSDEM.

---MORE---

## Rail travel

Railway unions have announced a strike notice from Sunday January 25th, 22:00
until Friday January 30th, 22:00. This may affect travel to Brussels for FOSDEM
and related fringe events.

While there will be a guaranteed minimum service in place, train frequency may
be significantly reduced. Also note that international connections might be
affected as well.

## Road travel
