The Beyond Blockchain - Distributed Web Devroom is a place to learn about and play with the open source infrastructure of the new internet. An emerging internet that is distributed, peer-to-peer, and secure. [Learn more here!](https://github.com/MorvinBear/Fosdem-Beyond-Blockchain-CfP) Our content supports the DWeb values: distributed > decentralized, data sovereignty, security, privacy, censorship-resistance, and generally being cool as humans. Sessions may include: * Q&As * demos * panels * workshops * presentations * on-boarding sessions * hands-on learning opportunities * talks that are maybe kind of weird and fun * inspiring multimedia arty experience for devs to “get” the distributed web
