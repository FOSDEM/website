This room is about building better software through a focus on testing and automation at all layers of the stack. From creating libraries and end-user applications all the way down to packaging, distribution and deployment. Testing and automation is not isolated to a single toolchain, language or platform, there is much to learn and share regardless of background!

This year the Testing and Automation devroom will host both lower level topics: hardware, bootloader, kernel as well as higher level topics: CI/CD, testing techniques, libraries and frameworks.

This is the place where open source projects can share their "testing from the trenches" stories!

Please check our CfP and submit a talk!
