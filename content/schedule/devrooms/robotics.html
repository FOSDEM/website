Welcome to the first edition of the Robotics and Simulation Developer room at FOSDEM, focused on core robotics libraries, frameworks, simulation tools, and open-source platforms. The selected talks will cover key tools used in Robotics,showcasing advancements and projects in the field. The half-day event will feature some full presentations and 2 lightning talk sessions, which will offer a platform for developers to share work and foster collaboration.
