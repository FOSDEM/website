Microkernels and component-based operating systems are pervasive parts of our computing landscape. They are currently used in situations where security and reliability are of utmost importance or where resources are scarce. The microkernel devroom brings the heads behind several projects together and offers a chance to get up to speed with developments in different groups.

Organization of the microkernel devroom is done in turns by groups in the microkernel community. This year's devroom is organized by the OS group of TU Dresden.
