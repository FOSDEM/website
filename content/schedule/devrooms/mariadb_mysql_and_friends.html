This devroom groups together the projects of the MariaDB& MySQL's ecosystem.

You will find talks related to MySQL, the world's most popular open source database. MySQL is free and open-source software under the terms of the GNU General Public License. MySQL is a relational database management system (RDMBS) but also a NoSQL Document Store.

Talks from MariaDB, that was initialy a fork of MySQL, also under GPL, now diverging to its own developement, will also be presented in this devroom.

And finally, Percona Server, also a fork of MySQL maintaining compatibility, is also discussed in the MySQL, MariaDB & Friends devroom.

Of course others open source products in the ecosystem of these open source databases have their place in the schedule as you can see !
