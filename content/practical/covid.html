The FOSDEM covid-19 policy is to:

  • implement all mandatory covid-19 measures
  • make it easy to follow government covid-19 recommendations

In practice, this tranlates to:

  • no mandatory covid-19 measures at FOSDEM 2023, barring last minute surprises
  • slightly less stands and physical rooms than at FOSDEM 2020
  • open/full rooms api
  • encouraging ventilation of developer rooms
  • FFP2 masks available on campus
