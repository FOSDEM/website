FOSDEM does not recommend any particular hotel or hostel. However, here is a list of popular accommodations for FOSDEM visitors to get you started. You can also check the mailing list for more information. wikivoyage also lists some places to stay.

Hotels

Brussels has a large selection of hotels in every price range.

FunKey

FunKey Hotel is a newly opened hotel in Brussels. For FOSDEM 2014, FunKey Hotel has decided to offer special prices for FOSDEM attendees, with the intent of turning it into a hotel dedicated to FOSDEM attendees during the first weekend of February.

Their offer includes the following:

  • 25 Euro per bed, per night
  • Breakfast
  • Wireless network
  • 24/7 snacking and beverages
  • Free parking
  • The use of their Chromebook laptops
  • The use of their mobile phones (with unlimited calls in Belgium)

They have the following fully equipped rooms available:

  • 9x 2-bed rooms
  • 18x 3-bed rooms
  • 7x 4-bed rooms

The offer of 25EUR/bed/night is valid for Friday, Saturday and Sunday night.

Please try to book as groups of 2, 3, or 4 friends or colleagues. It is also possible to book just one bed and share a room with people you don't know.

If you are interested, all you have to do is send an e-mail to Guillaume at guillaume@funkeyhotel.com, indicating:

  • Your name(s)
  • How many beds you wish to book
  • If you wish to book both Friday and Saturday night, or only Saturday night
  • If you wish to book Sunday night as well

People wishing to book both Friday and Saturday night have priority until 2013-10-01. This means that Saturday night only bookings will only be confirmed after this date. Bookings for both Friday an Saturday night can be paid immediately and will be instantly confirmed.

Payment must be made in advance and is nonrefundable. Guillaume will send you details on how to proceed after you have e-mailed him. You will have the possibility of paying either online with Visa, MasterCard or Bancontact (no American Express, Carte Bleue, Visa Electron or others) or by IBAN.

If the hotel is sufficiently booked by FOSDEM attendees by 2013-11-01, it will be declared a dedicated FOSDEM attendee hotel and bookings will be permanently closed for non-FOSDEM attendees. FOSDEM guests will then be able to use the common spaces of the hotel (breakfast room, meeting rooms, ...) at their leisure during their stay, providing for a convivial atmosphere and an unforgetable experience!

You can learn more about the FunKey here: http://funkeyhotel.com If you have any questions about this offer, please direct them to Guillaume at guillaume@funkeyhotel.com.

Please note that this is an offer from a third party extended to FOSDEM attendees. FOSDEM organisers have no relationship with FunKey. We only forward their offer to our attendees.
