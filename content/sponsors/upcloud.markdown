UpCloud is an independent European sovereign cloud provider with a
global footprint spanning four continents, including ten European data
centres. Founded in 2011, UpCloud is trusted by over 10,000 customers
worldwide, delivering reliable, performant, and cost-efficient
cloud-native services on proprietary-built infrastructure.

UpCloud champions the free and foundational spirit of the internet,
utilising open-source technologies where possible to ensure
interoperability, transparency, and freedom from vendor lock-in. By
integrating with and contributing to open-source projects, we help
drive innovation and enable flexible, future-proof cloud
architectures.

UpCloud supports the event financially.
