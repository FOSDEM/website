T-shirts and hoodies

We have T-shirts and hoodies again! As each year, they are a special limited edition to show our gratitude for you donating to FOSDEM. However, we are having a virtual conference this time, so the T-shirt process is a bit different from last year. For example, you don't have to queue and they won't run out this year.

T-shirts

The t-shirt design this year is grey with the FOSDEM 2021 logo. They're yours in exchange for a donation of €25.

Get them from here (EU) or here (outside of the EU).

Hoodies

The hoodie design this year is grey with the FOSDEM 2021 logo. They're yours in exchange for a donation of €50.

Get them from here (EU) or here (outside of the EU).

Getting them

For the online edition, we partnered with an print-on-demand provider, which means that they won't run out at Saturday noon, as they normally do.

Visitors from the EU can go to treasure.fosdem.org, offering shipping to the EU.

If you want to ship to a destionation outside of the EU, go to row.treasure.fosdem.org.

If you can't find your country in either of the shops or you can't use the provided payment methods, send an e-mail to treasure-at-fosdem-dot-org with how much hoodies/T-shirts you wish to order.

Delivery fees

The delivery fee depends on the country you wish to ship to. There are several tiers depending on what country you select. Unfortunately you can't see them before you select a country.
